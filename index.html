Research

We develop data‑driven computational approaches to understand, monitor, predict, and control neural dynamics, with applications in fundamental neuroscience, clinical neurology, and translational neuroengineering.

    Understand
    We develop data‑driven methods to characterize the structure, organization, and underlying mechanisms of large‑scale neural dynamics.

    Monitor
    We design algorithms that can detect and track subtle changes in underlying neuronal dynamics using neuroimaging data.

    Predict
    We construct predictive models to forecast cognitive outcomes and predict pathological events using features derived from neural dynamics.

    Control
    We establish data‑driven control strategies to modulate neural dynamics and steer brain states toward desired outcomes.
